## Content Strategy Overview

### Posting Cadence
- **3-4 Reels/week** (mix of funnel stages)
- **Best posting times** (BG audience): 19:00-21:00 weekdays, 10:00-12:00 weekends
- **Ratio per month**: 8 TOF : 6 MOF : 4 BOF

### Visual Identity
- Natural light, warm tones
- Brand palette: sage green (#B2D8C6), forest (#2D4A3E), blush (#FFC1CC), cream (#F4E3B2)
- Minimal aesthetic — no cluttered graphics
- Product always shown in lifestyle context (nightstand, kitchen counter, couch)
- Text overlays: clean sans-serif, white or forest green on semi-transparent backgrounds

### Audio Strategy
- Trending sounds when relevant, BUT original voiceover preferred for education content
- Soft lo-fi / acoustic guitar for ritual/aesthetic videos
- "Storytelling" voiceover tone — conversational, like talking to a friend

## Content Production Notes

### Equipment (Minimal Setup)
- **Camera**: iPhone 14+ or Samsung S23+ (4K, good low-light)
- **Lighting**: Ring light OR natural window light (golden hour preferred for warm tone)
- **Audio**: Clip-on lavalier mic for voiceover clarity (Boya BY-M1 or similar)
- **Backdrop**: Marble/wood surface, one plant, candle — keep it minimal

### Editing Tools
- **CapCut** — text overlays, transitions, auto-captions (supports Bulgarian)
- **InShot** — quick trimming, music layering
- Auto-captions: ALWAYS add — most viewers watch on mute

### Filming Tips
- Shoot vertical (9:16) always
- Keep main content in center 80% (safe zone for platform UI overlays)
- First frame must be visually interesting (it becomes the thumbnail on Reels)
- Cut every 2-3 seconds maximum to maintain attention
- Use jump cuts, not slow transitions
- Film multiple takes of the hook — it is the most important 3 seconds

## Key Principles (Do / Do Not)

### DO
- Speak like a friend, not a brand
- Lead with the PROBLEM, not the product
- Use clinical data as proof (27% cortisol, 13 studies, 300mg doses)
- Show the product in real-life context
- Be honest about what changed and what did not (Video 10)
- Add captions — always

### DO NOT
- Use "wellness guru" language ("manifest", "high vibe", "biohack")
- Promise cures or miracles
- Show before/after photos that look fake
- Use stock footage — everything should feel authentic
- Over-brand the TOF content — awareness videos should be 90% value, 10% brand
- Use aggressive sales language ("BUY NOW", "LIMITED OFFER")
